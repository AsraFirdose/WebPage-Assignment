Introduction

Mickey Mouse is more than just a cartoon character:he's a global icon loved by millions.

With his trademark ears and infectious smile,Mickey has become a symbol of happiness and joy.

History

Created by Walt Disney and Ub Iwerks,Mickey Mouse made his first appearance in the animated short film "Steamboat willie" in 1928.

Since then,Mickey has started in numerous films,television shows,and comic strips,becoming one of the most recognizable characters in the World.

Achievements

Mickey Mouse has achieved countless milestones throughout his illustrious career.

From recieving a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame to becoming the official mascot of The Walt Disney Company,Mickey's influence knows no bounds.
